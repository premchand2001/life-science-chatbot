Diabetes is a disease where blood sugar levels are too high.
Hypertension is another name for high blood pressure.
Fever is often a sign of infection or illness.
Blood sugar testing helps monitor diabetes.
Corona usually refers to coronavirus, a family of viruses that can cause respiratory illness.

COVID-19 is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus.

Common COVID-19 symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, and breathing difficulty.

Vaccines help reduce severe illness caused by COVID-19.
Cancer is a disease where abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the body.

Cancer can spread to other parts of the body through a process called metastasis.

Common cancer treatments include chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgery, and immunotherapy.

Early detection of cancer can improve treatment outcomes.
